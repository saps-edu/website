Common Roleplay Terms

Roleplay (RP)

The acting out or performance of a regular person in-game (e.g acting like a regular civilian, a gangster, Hispanic, African American, etc.)

In Character (IC)

In Character is your character's roleplay. This includes the actions and words your character says during roleplay.

Random Deathmatch (RDM)

Killing someone without giving them proper demands and it is a violation of GR 6.15.

Mercy Killing (MK)

Killing someone who is in an unconscious situation and it is a violation of GR 6.13.

Out of Character (OOC)

When you as a player communicate outside of roleplay. This includes talking in /b chat about things unrelated to your character.

Metagaming (MG)

Using information gained outside the game and using it inside the game. Example: telling your friend through Discord where your character is.

Powergaming (PG)

Doing unrealistic actions or forcing roleplay on others without giving them a chance to respond.

Deathmatching (DM)

Killing another player without a valid roleplay reason.

Non-RP

Actions that break the roleplay environment such as trolling, dancing in serious scenes, or ignoring realistic reactions.

Revenge Killing (RK)

Killing someone after respawning when your character should not remember the event.

Car Ramming

Using vehicles to intentionally crash into people or other vehicles.

Provoking

Purposely annoying or irritating other players to force a reaction.

Vehicle Deathmatch (VDM)

Killing or ramming players with your car.

Unrealistic Behavior (UB)

Actions that are unrealistic and negatively affect the roleplay experience.
